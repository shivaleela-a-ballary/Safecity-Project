App Purpose

CrimeWatch is designed to keep citizens informed about crime activity in their local area in real-time. It helps promote public safety by delivering timely alerts, visual crime trends, and neighborhood statistics.

Data Sources

Our data is sourced from verified feeds including local police departments, open crime data APIs, and community reports. We ensure high accuracy by filtering and cross-referencing inputs before displaying them.

Safety Tips

  • Stay aware of your surroundings, especially during late hours.
  • Always lock your doors and secure valuables.
  • Report suspicious activity to your local authorities.
  • Use well-lit, populated routes when walking at night.
